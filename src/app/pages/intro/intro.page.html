SLOEDP Live Election Results App!

Use the app to visualize the June 24th, 2023 Election results.

We also have historical Election Results from 1996 to 2018
  • Click the "Harmbuger Menu" in the top left corner to "Navigate to the various screens of the app"
  • Click the filter icon (...) located in the top right corner to "filter results by national, and district"
  • Click on the <--2018 or 2023--> links or swipe left or right to view results for that election year
  • Click on Table button located in the top right below the election year to view results in a table
  • Click the + symbol on the map to zoom into the map
  • Click the - symbol on the map to zoom out of the map
  • Click on Map button located in the top left below the election year to view results on the map
  • Click on a candidate name or photo to view their profile
  • Click on a political party name to view their profile
